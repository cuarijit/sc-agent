# Key Takeaways — Executive Summary

UND's opportunity is not capacity or supply — it is **execution precision and earlier, value-optimal decisions**. Puls8 delivers both, with Agentic AI as the decisive differentiator.

---

## The Two Challenges

- **Route-to-Market Execution** — plans do not translate into what actually sells; trucks are loaded by judgment, ~10% returns unsold.
- **Milk Utilisation & Allocation** — supply is known, but utilisation is reactive; decisions are made *after*, not *earlier*.

---

## The Puls8 Response — Four Layers

- **Layer 1 — Baseline Planning:** clean signals, segmented forecasting, route/SKU granularity.
- **Layer 2 — Demand Sensing:** hybrid statistical + ML for core SKUs, probabilistic for long-tail.
- **Layer 3 — Inventory Optimisation:** dynamic, service-level-driven safety stock — reduce waste **and** stockout.
- **Layer 4 — Agentic AI *(differentiator)*:** continuous scan, diagnose, and recommend at route and node level.

The first three stabilise the baseline. The fourth is where UND pulls ahead of peers.

---

## Agentic AI — Answering UND's Pain Points Directly

| Customer Pain Point | Agent | What It Delivers | Measurable Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *"Plan is not translating to what sells — trucks loaded by experience"* | **UND Route-to-Market Sensing Agent** | Real-time POS divergence, per-truck SKU mix, returns diagnosis by route | Returns **10% → <5%**; sell-through lift on priority routes |
| *"We need to decide earlier what to do with the milk, not after"* | **UND Milk Utilisation & Allocation Diagnostic Agent** | Daily milk-pool diagnosis, optimal product mix, expiry and excess risk flagged 48h ahead | Decisions shift from **reactive → proactive**; spoilage and write-off value reduced |

Both agents operate on a **Prompt → Intent → Action** pattern — a planner asks a question, the agent identifies the SKU, location, root cause, and value-optimal action.

---

## Business Outcomes at a Glance

- **Speed** — issue detection moves from days to hours.
- **Precision** — loadability resolved at SKU × route level.
- **Value** — waste and stockout reduced *together*, not traded off.
- **Proactivity** — allocation decided earlier, before value leaks.
- **Productivity** — planners supervise agents rather than chase data.

---

## Recommended Next Steps

- **Pilot** the two UND agents on a focused Riyadh route cluster and the daily milk pool for 6–8 weeks.
- **Instrument** the pilot with three KPIs: route returns %, sell-through %, and milk spoilage value.
- **Scale** on proven uplift — extend to the national route network and full product mix.
